Multiplayer WebVR Demo

Before you start

This game works with Oculus Rift, Google Cardboard v1 and Google Cardboard v2.

To get the best experience, please tell me what device you are using

Google Cardboard v2

The Cardboard v2 and its clones have a touch button that is reachable from the top of the Cardboard. You can identify this by looking at the top of your Cardboard.
If there is at least one button visible on the top, you've got a Cardboard v2.

Press and hold the button to move

Click here to start the demo

Google Cardboard v1

The Cardboard v1 and its clones have no touch button. You can identify this by looking at the top of your Cardboard.
If there is no button visible on either side, you've got a Cardboard v1.

You will automatically start moving

Click here to start the demo

Oculus Rift

For Oculus Rift the only tested version is the DK 2, but it should work with the DK 1 as well. You will need a WebVR capable browser and the Oculus Runtime.

You will automatically start moving

Click here to start the demo
